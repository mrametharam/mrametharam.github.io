Percent vs Pixels

Percent is relative to its container, while pixel is fixed relative to the page.

50%
100px
50%
100px

Percent vs VW vs VH

VW and VH are the same as the percent, except that they are always relative to the page and not their container.

50%
50vw
25vh
50%
50vw
25vh

REM vs EM

REM & EM are percent sizes. 1r/em = 100%
REM: relative to the root.
EM: relative to the parent.

1em
